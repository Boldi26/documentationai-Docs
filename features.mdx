## Event Creation

Creating an event on Kucska Bolka is intuitive and powerful. You can add multiple photos, set detailed descriptions, specify time zones, and configure privacy settings.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Event Creator" icon="edit">
    Navigate to the 'Create Event' section in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Details" icon="file-text">
    Enter title, description, date, location, and category.
    
    <CodeGroup tabs="JSON,XML">
      ````json
{
  "title": "Winter Ball 2024",
  "description": "Annual university winter dance",
  "date": "2024-12-15T19:00:00Z",
  "location": "Grand Hall",
  "category": "party",
  "maxAttendees": 300
}
````
      ````xml
<event>
  <title>Winter Ball 2024</title>
  <description>Annual university winter dance</description>
  <date>2024-12-15T19:00:00Z</date>
  <location>Grand Hall</location>
  <category>party</category>
  <maxAttendees>300</maxAttendees>
</event>
````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="send">
    Review and publish your event to the community.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Smart Discovery

Our intelligent discovery system helps you find events that match your interests. Filters include date ranges, categories, and proximity.

| Feature | Description | Example |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| Location-based | Find events near you | Within `2 km` of campus |
| Category filters | Party, study, sports | Filter by `party` |
| Interest matching | Based on past RSVPs | Similar to events you've attended |

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="How does interest matching work?" default-open="false">
    Our algorithm analyzes your RSVP history and suggests events you might enjoy, increasing engagement by up to 40%.
  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="Can I save favorite categories?" default-open="false">
    Yes, create custom filters and save them for quick access to your preferred event types.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
